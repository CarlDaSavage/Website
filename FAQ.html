Do I need to have a Rubik's Cube to join the club?

You will not need to have a Rubik's Cube to join the club! We believe that the club should be accessible to everyone and anyone who would like to join. Because of this, we will be offering our members an oppurtunity to buy a Rubik's Cube from us for $_. If you'd only like to borrow one, with a $_ deposit, we will gladly lend you one!

Do I need to know how to solve a Rubik's Cube?

Not at all! Our main focus of making the Cubing Club was actually to teach people how to solve the Rubik's Cube (as well as other different types of Rubik's Cubes)! You do not need any prior expirience to join the Cubing Club!
